<center>Munchy is a meal planner and stocktake application that will track calories and assist with your shopping list</center>

<br>
<br>

# Features

### Profiles
- Create a profile to track your personal nutritional requirements
- Shopping with others? Add additional profiles
- Think of the application as a hub for the household, and the profiles are each member
    
### Consumables

- Add food or beverage items to your library of consumables
- Each item can contain multiple products, these should be listed as brand/variant (e.g. Item: Soda - Brand/Variant: CocaCola Classic(12oz))
- Then each product should include (Quantity, Carbs, Protein, Fat, and Avg Price)

### Recipes
- Recipes can be created using items from your consumables library
- This allows you to easily add in common meals quickly to your planner
- Frequent a resturant regularly? Store your favourite meals to track macros

### Planner
- Add recipes or consumables to your weekly planner
- Choose to repeat the current day (Weekly, Fortnightly, Monthly, Everyday, or Today only)
- Two displays will appear to show your proposed macros and your target macros

### Pantry
- Take stock of the current consumables in your pantry
- Set up a soft warning that shows on your planner if your pantry cannot supply the next x number of days

### Shopping list
- Select the number of days that you would like to shop for
- Munchy will take account of your current stock and the timeline provided to source you a list of required consumables
